SoundBridge

SoundBridge is a social platform for artists and music enthusiasts alike. It was developed with one thing in mind: Connecting Artists and Listeners in a tight-knit community. Not only are artists able to post and share updates about concerts and upcoming projects. Listeners to, are encouraged to have a voice about the music they love and artists they listen to. By sharing their opinions on set lists and tour locations artists are able to accelerate their fan turnouts but also increase their roi per concert. This in turn develops a stronger bond between artists and music lovers around the world connecting them all in one place. Listeners are also encouraged to connect with like minded users who enjoy a similar taste in music. With the ability to post and share curated playlists soundbridge is a place where melomaniacs alike discover amazing music. This is SoundBridge.
